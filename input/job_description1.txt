Job description for mechanical engineer position:
We are seeking a skilled Mechanical Engineer to join our team.
The ideal candidate will have a strong background in mechanical design, analysis, and project management.
Responsibilities include designing mechanical systems, conducting simulations, and collaborating with cross-functional teams to ensure project success.
A degree in Mechanical Engineering and proficiency in CAD software are required. Experience with thermal systems and fluid dynamics is a plus.
Strong problem-solving skills and attention to detail are essential.
